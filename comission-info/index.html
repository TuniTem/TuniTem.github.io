Commission info

Information on what to expect from commissioning me!

If you like what I do and want to work together, or just have any questions either DM me on discord, twitter, (@tunitem) or you can fill out this forum, and I'll get back to you!

If you commission me, I'll make a specialized application specifically tailored to your preferences, hardware, and streams- with a variable amount of features depending on your budget/needs. After delivery, I'll provide bugfix support and be on call to help with any issues that arise, so that your overlay can seamlessly integrate into your stream and world.
