Engine Overlays for Streams

All stream overlays are programmed in the Godot Game Engine

A star for every follower - with each having their own permanent place on stream. Stars form constellations, and constellations can work together to form permanent shapes in dreamspace! Every quality of the star (color, orientation, location and more) is determined ​ ​ ​ ​ by each followers’ unique username. ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ Followers can nudge their stars to ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ change the overall shape of the ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ constellation into whatever they can ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ ​ coordinate!

Overlay Tool (OT) is a Godot library I'm building that will let me do some pretty cool things! This tool is shared and regularly updated with new stuff that I need for projects - so that naturally as I continue to add more features and compatibility, all of my previous overlays will receive those new features or ​ ​ tools as well!

Though this isn't in the current version, ​ I plan to add easy to use tools in OT ​ ​ that will allow you to create your own ​ screens and gizmos - all without code!

With Godot I am able to bring to life pretty much anything you can imagine when it comes to alerts and screens! I find that they can be a whole lot more than what you usually see. For example, I like to have fun with my overlay - if I get some idea for a game I'm streaming soon, I might make a bespoke screen or widget I can just plug in! With OverlayTool, I give that power to you as well!

I did not make the games in the ​ ​ ​ background, credit goes to the original ​ ​ creators

Why an engine overlay?

< Creative freedom >
You’re not limited to prebuilt templates or cookie-cutter aesthetics like you are with typical overlays. I'm able to do anything from a funny info widget to a full 3D environment set in your world. You'll have an application developed specifically for you, how cool is that?

< Increased interaction >
An interactive chat game with lasting effects adds a unique and fun addition to your streams that keeps viewers coming back.

< Growing ecosystem >
As time goes on, you will receive updates, personal bugfixes, and major new features - all free of charge.
